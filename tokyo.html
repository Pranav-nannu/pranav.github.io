Tokyo, Japan

Overview

Tokyo, the bustling capital of Japan, is a city that seamlessly blends tradition and modernity. From ancient temples to futuristic skyscrapers, Tokyo offers a unique cultural experience. Visitors can explore historic sites like the Meiji Shrine and Senso-ji Temple, while also enjoying vibrant neighborhoods such as Shibuya and Akihabara.
