Rafisah Dam, Pakistan

Overview

Rafisah Dam, located in the northern region of Pakistan, is a magnificent engineering marvel that serves as a vital source of water for irrigation and hydroelectric power generation. This impressive structure offers a serene environment for visitors to enjoy the surrounding natural beauty and learn about the region's rich history.
